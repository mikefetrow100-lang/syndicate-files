Case Summary

Isadore "Marty" Kowalski, 44, known associate of the Minneapolis Syndicate, was found deceased in the Mississippi River near the Hennepin Ave bridge on the morning of October 14, 1958. Death ruled accidental drowning. Case closed within 48 hours by Det. Frank Ohlsen — later found to be on the Syndicate payroll.

New evidence suggests Kowalski had been skimming from Syndicate bookmaking operations and was planning to disappear with the money. Someone found out. Someone made sure he didn't make it.
